Instructions

  • 1. Fill you air tank at your local paint ball store to allow for full power

  • 2. Attach the air tank to the back by using an appropriate wrench to fit your air tank

  • 3. Fill the hopper with up to 8 ping pong balls

  • 4. Pull the reload handle back and push it forward to drop a ball into the barrel

  • 5. Open the Valve for about half a second and then close it to fire the ping pong ball

  • 6. Repeat steps 3-5 for endless fun!

Safety

  • 1. Wear eye protection at all times!

  • 2. Do not cause severe impact to the tank
